Initial Prototyping

Our process began with a broad exploration based on the goal of helping choreographers better visualize group choreography. Initial ideas included gesture based tools, video tools, as well as creation tools centered around alowing choreographers to create useful visualizations of their choreography.

Prototyping - Second Round

In our second round of more refined prototypes, we created a mix of feasibility and usability prototypes around two concepts: the video loop machine, which we made a skit for in P2, and a formation visualizer for the iPad based around gestures, which we paper prototyped. Also, in a feasilbility prototype, Jay created a quick app to see that detecting multiple fingers was possible

Wizard of Oz Prototyping

After discussion, we as a team decided to move forward with the formation visualizer concept. We paper prototyped two different interface ideas: one with a more traditional UI very similar to creative tools such as photoshop, and a second interface which was much more gestural.
