Team Dance

The need we are addressing is the need for choreographers to visualize the dynamics involved with creating group choreographies and visualizing them in action. At present, the process of coming up with a group choreography is very much like a black-box to the choreographer because the choreographer does not know whether it looks good or not until it is taught to the dancers. Unfortunately, this is usually very late in the process because the dancers have already practised the steps and it is difficult to re-teach them new moves. Choreographers really need to feel confident that the steps and formations that they are coming up with will look good and if not, they need to know early enough. Our class project will focus on solving this real problem faced by choreographers.
